Privacy Policy

This Rice Purity Test values your privacy and collects only minimal, non-identifiable data to support anonymous research and analytics.

What We Collect

  • Your answers to the 100-question test
  • Your final purity score
  • The time and date of your submission
  • General location data (e.g. country and or state), inferred from your IP address

What We Don't Collect

  • No names, email addresses, or accounts
  • No cookies, login info, or tracking IDs
  • No precise or stored IP addresses

How We Use the Data

Submission data is stored anonymously and used for statistical analysis, research, and educational insights. Aggregate data may be published or shared, but never in a way that could identify individuals.

Your Consent

By submitting your responses, you agree to the collection and use of this data as described above. If you do not wish for your submission to be stored, please do not complete the test.

This policy may be updated periodically. Last updated: October 2025.
